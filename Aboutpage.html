About Us

We intend to make Farmers' lives easier and the agricultural environment more fruitful and rich. Using our Carbon Footprint Tracker, Farmers can search different places and selectively plant their crops accordingly. This ensures eco-friendly farming. Our Water Availability System also allows farmers to select places with high water levels underground. Our Soil Management System checks if the soil in an entered region is fertile enough to plant.
