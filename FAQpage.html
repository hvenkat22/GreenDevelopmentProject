FAQs



How can I make my property more environmentally friendly?

You can enter your address and our Carbon FootPrint Tracker will give the Footprint Value and based on that value, you can either change the address to select a different location to plant your crops or select the entered address.


What innovative technologies can help me reduce my environmental footprint and increase productivity?

Our Water Availability System tracks the water level underground. And our Soil Management System would check for Soil Fertility for Optimum Farming.


How can I improve the health and fertility of my soil without relying on synthetic chemicals?

Our Soil Management system measures the Fertility but you can also Rotate your crops, perform composting, or use natural minerals like Limestone or Phosphate Rock.
